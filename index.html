AI-Powered Travel Planning Project

This project started with a simple problem: I didn't want to spend my holiday making hundreds of tiny decisions. Using Claude, I built a bilingual travel field guide and a cultural companion handbook for a 7-day journey across Sicily, Malta and Crete.

Lessons Learned

  • AI is powerful, but verification matters.
  • Human-in-the-loop beats blind automation.
  • Constraints often improve the final output.
  • The best travel memories are usually the parts no AI can predict.
